The Person Behind the Work

Meet Steve

Steve, owner of SG Assured Renovations, has been working in construction since he was 16. Originally from Owen Sound, Ontario, he learned early that if a job's worth doing, it's worth doing right — a work ethic that's stayed with him across a career spanning more than 40 years.

As a union carpenter and concrete former, Steve has worked across Toronto, the GTA, Kingston, and Ottawa. He's helped build high-rise buildings, supervised municipal water treatment facilities, worked at Hydro One transformer stations, and contributed to the Parliament Hill Heritage Restoration Project and the Ottawa LRT — alongside countless homes, renovations, additions, and foundations.

Today he and his wife are restoring a century-old home in Edwardsburgh Cardinal — a daily reminder of what homeowners go through when they renovate, and why good workmanship matters. This isn't a company with salespeople or subcontractors. When you call, you're talking directly with Steve, and he's the one doing the work.

  • 35 years union-trained carpenter & concrete former
  • Residential, commercial, industrial & municipal work
  • Knowledge of the Ontario Building Code
  • Water management, drainage & foundation protection
— Steve
